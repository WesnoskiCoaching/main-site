Questions before you apply

Straight answers.

What does coaching cost?
This is a serious 1-on-1 engagement, not an app subscription. Investment is case-by-case and depends on the tier of access and program length that fits your situation. Exact numbers are covered transparently on the discovery call.
Are you a doctor?
No. I'm a certified health coach and I never position myself as a physician. I don't diagnose or prescribe. Any medication decision belongs to you and your physician.
My doctor said my labs are normal. How is this different?
Standard reference ranges are built from population averages, including sick populations. Functional ranges ask a different question: is this marker where a thriving body runs. A TSH of 4.2 is "normal" and can still explain your fatigue, hair loss, and cold hands.
What if it doesn't work?
Section 7.3 of every contract is a conditional performance guarantee. Complete 8 consecutive weeks at 100% compliance and if the marker we're working on hasn't moved, full refund of your coaching investment. Proof of compliance provided on request.
What happens after I apply?
I read every application personally. If it looks like a fit, you'll get a booking link within 48 hours for a real conversation with our Client Success Director. If it's not a fit, we'll tell you that too, and point you toward what will help.
